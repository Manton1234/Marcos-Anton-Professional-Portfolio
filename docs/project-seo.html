Marketplace Listing Strategy

Optimizing listings, pricing, photos, and follow-up for local car buyer demand.

Approach

This project treats Facebook Marketplace and local listing platforms as a search engine for vehicle demand. The strategy ranks listings by photo quality, price competitiveness, buyer intent, response speed, and inspection proof.

Listing TypeBuyer IntentLead QualityAction
Clean-title SUVFamily and commuterHighBoost listing and feature service history
Fuel-efficient sedanBudget and studentHighLead with monthly cost and reliability
TruckWork and utilityMedium-highShow payload, service, and test-drive CTA
Luxury vehicleStatus and comfortMediumRetarget with trust proof and inspection details

Recommendation

Use a listing checklist for every vehicle: clean exterior photo, interior proof, mileage, title status, recent work completed, inspection summary, transparent price, and fast message response. This turns Arg Tech operations into buyer confidence.
